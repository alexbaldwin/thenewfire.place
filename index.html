The future of fireplaces.

Sitting around the fire is a major part of the human existence. We gather around to tell big stories and sing songs. Yet, the overall experience of fireplaces hasn't changed in the last millenia. Until now, you've had to manually spark the kindling and stoke the coals. Never again will you have to laboriously tend to your fireplace.

This is the new fireplace. This is where fireplaces are heading.
